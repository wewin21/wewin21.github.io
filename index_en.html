Core Features

Record Dealt Cards

Record cards dealt in each round, count the number of each suit and card value, display win/loss outcomes under optimal decisions, providing intuitive visualization for pattern analysis.

Various Metrics Calculation

Integrates Hi-Lo, Hi-OptII, OmegaII card counting systems, calculates high-low card true counts in real-time, helping players adjust bet sizes and strategies.

Hand Probability Calculation

Calculate the probability of hands like pairs, straights, flushes, and 19-21 points based on the remaining deck, providing scientific decision-making basis.

Bust Probability Calculation

Accurately calculate the probability of busting based on player and dealer hands, providing advantageous recommendations on whether to hit or stand.

Expected Value Comparison

Calculate the player's expected value when "Hitting" or "Standing". Through scientific comparison, help select the optimal strategy.
